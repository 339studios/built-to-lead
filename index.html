Our Vision

Leaders Who Build Teams

Built to Lead exists to close the gap between operational talent and leadership capability. Too often, our best performers become our newest managers — and then they're expected to lead without ever being taught how.

This program gives Red Wing retail leaders a structured, practical foundation in what it actually means to lead people: to set standards, coach consistently, have hard conversations, and build teams that perform even when no one is watching.

Aligned with the Triple Stitch Strategy, Built to Lead ensures that leadership excellence becomes a competitive advantage — not a variable — across every store in the network.

Built to Lead develops leaders who don't just manage stores — they build teams.

The Program

Three Phases. Nine Modules. One Goal.

Phase 1
The Leader Within
Modules 1–3

Mindset, identity, and the fundamentals of leading yourself before leading others.

Phase 2
The Leader with Others
Modules 4–6

Coaching, feedback, and the skills required to drive high performance in people.

Phase 3
The Leader Who Builds
Modules 7–9

Culture, talent development, and leading through change at scale.

Sustainability

Built to Last Beyond the Classroom

How Learning is Measured

  • Module reflections submitted after each session
  • Application exercises + manager check-ins every two weeks
  • Peer discussion + structured debrief in group sessions
  • Capstone presentation of personal leadership commitments
  • Manager observation and field coaching notes

How Learning is Reinforced

  • Weekly coaching log practice built into the program rhythm
  • Leadership habit cards for daily on-the-floor use
  • Monthly district touchpoints for ongoing accountability
  • Annual alumni refresh to revisit and deepen core skills
  • Frameworks embedded directly into store SOPs and routines
